codersWhoLift()

Featured Project.

codersWhoLift() is a fitness application created to enhance the accessibility and experience of our fellow gym-goers. Designed to track the users stats, exercises, and information, while providing instructional videos to help with form and understanding, this application not only the more advanced gym-goer, but also the amateur.

My role within the group was working on the YouTube API, allowing access to the various YouTube videos that went along with the exercise the user wanted information on.
